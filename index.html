Who we are? 

| THE STRAT COLLECTIVE

The Strat Collective is a multidisciplinary consultancy that uses behavior science to understand and analyze human behavior to solve strategic questions for brands, business and community. 



Using proprietary tools we connect the dots between people, culture, and content. Our Conviction Discovery Framework puts curiosity at the center. It asks questions around our memory structures, decodes social norms, delves into the semiotics and existing vocabulary construct, draws on visual culture while mapping consumer journey to inform the solution narrative.
